About Us

Kamogelo Makgatholle is the visionary founder and director of EmissionTrack, a pioneering carbon accounting solution that is transforming the way businesses measure, manage, and reduce their environmental impact. With a unique blend of expertise in economics, information systems, and data science, Makgatholle is uniquely positioned to tackle the complex challenges of sustainability and emissions management. Makgatholle's educational background includes a Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) degree, with majors in Economics and Information Systems, as well as a Data Science certification. This comprehensive training has equipped her with a deep understanding of business operations, data-driven decision-making, and the application of advanced analytics to solve real-world problems..
